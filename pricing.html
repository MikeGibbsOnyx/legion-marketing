Questions?

Do tokens expire?

Token packs never expire. Subscription tokens reset monthly.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel your subscription anytime. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards via Stripe. Secure and encrypted.

Is there a refund policy?

Subscriptions can be canceled anytime. Token packs are non-refundable once purchased.

Can I use my own API keys?

Yes! BYOK (Bring Your Own Keys) is available in Settings. Use your own API keys for unlimited queries at your own cost.

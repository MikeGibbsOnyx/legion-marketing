Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Onyx AI Labs Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") operates Onyx Legion, a multi-AI deliberation platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and authentication credentials. For phone authentication, we collect and verify your phone number.

Usage Data

We collect information about your interactions with our platform, including queries submitted, AI models selected, token usage, and response preferences.

Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store complete credit card numbers. We retain transaction IDs, amounts, and subscription status.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To process transactions and manage subscriptions
  • To send service announcements and administrative messages
  • To respond to your requests and provide customer support
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • AI Model Providers: Your queries are sent to third-party AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, xAI, DeepSeek) to generate responses. Each provider has their own privacy policy.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google), Stripe (payments), and SendGrid (email) to operate our platform.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information and conversation history for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Settings page. Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (TLS), encrypted data at rest, secure authentication (JWT tokens), and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your conversation history
  • Opt out of marketing communications

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the platform.

About Onyx Legion

We believe the best answers come from multiple perspectives. That's why we built the first multi-AI deliberation platform.

The Problem

AI is incredible. But every model has blind spots. ChatGPT hallucinates. Claude hedges. Gemini oversimplifies. When you ask one AI a question, you get one perspective—with all its biases and limitations.

Power users know this. They open multiple tabs, copy-paste the same question to different AIs, and manually compare the answers. It works, but it's tedious.

We asked: what if the AIs could do that comparison themselves? What if they could debate, challenge each other, and refine their answers before you see them?

The Solution

Onyx Legion queries five leading AI models—Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, Grok, and DeepSeek—and runs them through a structured 4-round deliberation process:

  1. Independent answers — Each AI responds without seeing the others
  2. Cross-examination — They critique each other's responses
  3. Refinement — They incorporate valid criticism and defend correct points
  4. Synthesis — A final answer combines the best of all perspectives

The result is more accurate, more balanced, and more trustworthy than any single model can produce alone.
